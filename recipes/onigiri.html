Onigiri

Ingredients

  • kosher/sea salt
  • 4 sheets of seaweed
  • salted salmon
  • okaka
  • tuna mayo
  • 3 umeboshi (Japanese picked plum)
  • seasoned kombu
  • toasted white and black sesame seeds

Steps

  1. Cut the nori sheets in thirds
  2. First, wet both of your hands with water in order to prevent the rice from sticking to your hands
  3. Scoop out a handful of warm rice into one hand. Create a small well in the center of the rice. Put one kind of filling inside. Then mold the rice with your hands around the well to cover your filling completely
  4. Press the rice around the filling to gently form the rice into a triangle.
  5. Wrap the onigiri with nori seaweed
  6. Place a little bit of each filling on top of onigiri so we know which kind it is.

Happy eating!
